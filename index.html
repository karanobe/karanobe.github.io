About Me

Hi, I’m a frontend developer who loves creating clean, engaging, and easy-to-use websites that make people’s lives a little smoother. I’ve worked on everything from high-converting sales funnels to sleek online storefronts, always with an eye for great design and a smooth user experience.

When I’m not at my desk, you’ll probably find me on a basketball court, a pickleball court, or a tennis court (basically, if it has a net, I’m in). I bring the same energy and teamwork from the court into every project I work on—minus the sweat.
